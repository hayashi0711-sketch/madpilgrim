# MAD Pilgrim Strategy Pivot

Last updated: 2026-06-18

## Positioning

MAD Pilgrim will not compete primarily for the generic `聖地巡礼` category.

The product is redefined around:

1. **Filming-location tourism**
   - filming locations
   - screen tourism
   - ロケ地観光
   - 映像作品を起点にした旅

2. **Screen-to-table food experiences**
   - food shown or referenced in a work
   - the real filming restaurant or cafe
   - an official collaboration menu
   - a model or regional equivalent that can still be eaten

## Editorial Promise

Answer two concrete questions:

- Where was this scene filmed or visually modeled?
- Where can I eat the food connected to this work?

Nearby restaurants without a documented relationship to the work are not the
primary product.

## Data Safety

- Wikidata records are candidate leads, never verified publishing records.
- Automated sources enter a private staging table with source IDs and relation
  metadata.
- Human verification and evidence are required before publication.
- City-level narrative settings are not presented as exact filming locations.

## UI Reset

Previous generated concepts and the Urban Pilgrimage direction are rejected.

The new UI must be designed from zero for a fashion-forward screen-tourism and
food editorial product. It must not visually reference MAD about U mockups or
the discarded MAD Pilgrim concepts.
